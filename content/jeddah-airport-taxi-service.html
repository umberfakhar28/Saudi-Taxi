Jeddah Airport Terminal Guide

King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED) serves as the main gateway for pilgrims visiting Makkah and Madinah. Our service covers all three terminals with specific meet & greet procedures for each.

Terminal 1

New International Terminal

Flights:

International flights from major airlines including Saudia, Emirates, Qatar Airways, Turkish Airlines, and others.

Meeting Point:

Arrival Hall - Exit through Gates 5-6. Your driver will wait with a name board at the designated taxi meeting area.

Facilities:

  • Modern immigration and customs
  • Baggage claim carousels 1-8
  • Currency exchange
  • SIM card vendors (STC, Mobily, Zain)
  • Restaurants and cafes
  • Prayer facilities

Hajj Terminal

Pilgrimage Terminal

Flights:

Seasonal terminal operating during Hajj season (Dhul-Hijjah). Dedicated to Hajj pilgrims with streamlined immigration.

Meeting Point:

Main Arrival Hall - Large open area with designated group meeting zones. Look for driver with name board.

Special Services:

  • Dedicated Hajj immigration counters
  • Ihram assistance
  • Group coordination
  • Luggage assistance for pilgrim groups
  • Zamzam water distribution
  • Direct to Makkah/Madinah transport

South Terminal

Dom Terminal

Flights:

Domestic flights within Saudi Arabia operated by Saudia, Flynas, and Flyadeal. Also some regional flights.

Meeting Point:

Exit Lobby - After baggage claim, exit to the main lobby area. Driver waits with name board near exit doors.

Facilities:

  • Domestic baggage claim
  • Quick exit procedures
  • Food court
  • Retail shops
  • Car rental desks

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Jeddah Airport to Makkah?

Taxi from King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED) to Makkah costs between SAR 150-400 depending on vehicle type:

  • Economy Sedan: SAR 150-200
  • Family SUV: SAR 250-350
  • Business Van: SAR 400+

Where do I meet my driver at Jeddah Airport?

Terminal 1: Exit through Gates 5-6, look for driver with name board in arrival hall.

Hajj Terminal: Main arrival hall, designated meeting zones.

South Terminal: Exit lobby after baggage claim.

What if my flight is delayed?

We track your flight in real-time. Your driver will be informed of any delays and will wait up to 2 hours after your scheduled landing time at no extra charge.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, child and infant seats are available free of charge. Please specify the age of your child when booking so we can provide the appropriate seat.

Can I pay in cash?

Yes, we accept cash (SAR) on arrival. We also accept credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Online bookings require card payment.

How early should I book?

For regular days: 24-48 hours in advance. During Ramadan: 1 week ahead. During Hajj season: 2-4 weeks recommended.

Do you serve all Jeddah Airport terminals?

Yes, we serve all three terminals: Terminal 1 (International), Hajj Terminal (Seasonal), and South Terminal (Domestic). Specify your terminal when booking.

Is there WiFi in the vehicles?

WiFi is available in Business Van and luxury vehicle categories. Standard vehicles do not have WiFi but drivers can assist with local SIM card purchases at the airport if needed.

About King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED)

Airport Information

  • IATA Code: JED
  • Location: 19 km north of Jeddah city center
  • Distance to Makkah: 100 km (60-90 min)
  • Distance to Madinah: 420 km (4-5 hours)
  • Terminals: 3 (Terminal 1, Hajj, South)
  • Annual Passengers: 40+ million

Arrival Procedures

  1. Disembark from aircraft
  2. Proceed to immigration counters
  3. Present passport and visa
  4. Collect luggage from baggage claim
  5. Clear customs (if applicable)
  6. Exit to arrival hall
  7. Meet your driver

Arrival Tips

  • Have passport and visa ready
  • Fill out arrival card (if required)
  • Keep baggage claim tags
  • Purchase local SIM card for data
  • Exchange currency if needed
  • Download offline maps
