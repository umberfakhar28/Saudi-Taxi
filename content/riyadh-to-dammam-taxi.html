Route Details: Riyadh to Dammam

Journey Information

  • Distance: 400 kilometers (248 miles)
  • Duration: 4 hours (non-stop)
  • Route: Highway 40 (primary business corridor)
  • Road Quality: Excellent 6-lane highway
  • Tolls: SAR 15-20
  • Fuel Stops: Available every 50-80 km

Key Stops Along Route

  • Al Kharj (100km): Rest facilities, restaurants
  • Al Ahsa region (250km): Major rest area
  • Highway Service Stations: Multiple stops with food, fuel, prayer

Best Travel Times

  • Weekday mornings: 6:00-7:00 AM (arrive before business hours)
  • Avoid: Thursday evenings (5:00-9:00 PM) - heavy weekend traffic
  • Friday: Lighter traffic during Jumu'ah prayer time
